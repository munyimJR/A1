Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

: Emphasizing technology and research to foster new ideas and solutions, particularly in sectors like agriculture, textiles, and IT.

Sustainability

Focusing on environmental protection, renewable energy, and sustainable practices to ensure a healthy and prosperous future for all citizens.

Infrastructure

Investing in transportation, communication, and urban development to improve connectivity and quality of life.

Empowerment

Promoting gender equality, education, and economic opportunities to empower all citizens, especially women and marginalized communities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.
